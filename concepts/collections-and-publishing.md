# Collections and publishing

**Status: stub.** Part of the [Concepts](README.md) section.

## What this page covers

**Collections** are user-curated groupings of records — like a folder, but cross-cutting (one record can sit in many collections; a collection can mix any objecttypes). **Publishing** turns a collection (or specific records) into a public URL.
